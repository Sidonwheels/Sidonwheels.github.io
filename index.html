About

The Story Behind the Lens

↗ Watch Channel

I'm Siddharth Bhalla, an adventure filmmaker and self-taught video editor based in India. For over 5 years, I've been behind the wheel and behind the edit bay — documenting raw, cinematic road trips across India's most challenging and stunning terrains.

My channel SiD On Wheels isn't just travel content — it's episodic storytelling. Every video goes through a full post-production pipeline: footage culling, multi-track editing, color grading, sound design, and export optimization. I've done this 566 times, consistently, independently, and on deadline.

What sets me apart as an editor is an instinct for narrative pacing — knowing when to let a wide shot breathe and when to cut hard to keep energy alive. Clean timelines, fast turnarounds, and storytelling that holds attention.

I specialize in travel & adventure content, automotive films, cinematic vlogs, drone footage integration, and episodic series editing. Available for freelance projects and full-time roles.
