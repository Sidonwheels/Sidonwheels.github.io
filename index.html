Background

The Story Behind the Lens


I'm Siddharth Bhalla, an adventure filmmaker and self-taught video editor based in India. For over 3 years, I've been behind the wheel and behind the edit bay — documenting raw, cinematic road trips across India's most challenging and stunning terrains.

My channel SiD On Wheels isn't just travel content — it's episodic storytelling. Every video goes through a full post-production pipeline: footage culling, multi-track editing, colour grading, sound design, and export optimisation. I've done this 566 times, consistently, independently, and on deadline.

What sets me apart is an instinct for narrative pacing — knowing when to let a wide shot breathe and when to cut hard to keep energy alive. Clean timelines, fast turnarounds, and storytelling that holds attention.
